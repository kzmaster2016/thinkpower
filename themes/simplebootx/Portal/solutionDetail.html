With the emergency of sharing device,
such as sharing bike,sharing car,the GPS
is helping the sharing company to manage the devices remotely.

How could LOT to support you?

  • Find the Device Fast:

    It helps the user to find device fast,and the company to know the exiting of the current whole devices.

  • Open the Lock Instantly:

    The user could use the Bluetooth or the SIM network to open the around device by themselves.

  • Device Location Optimal Management:

    The GPS history report will offer data to the device company on how to locate the device in the neaded area.

  • User Behavior Management:

    The payment of user will be decided by the using time,while the open and closure of the lock will help to decide the using time.
